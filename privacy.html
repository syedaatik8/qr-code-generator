Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy and data security.

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Dr. QR Code. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we handle your information when you use our online QR code generation tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

Information We Don't Collect

Dr. QR Code is designed with privacy as a core principle. Our tool operates entirely within your browser, which means:

  • Your data is never uploaded to our servers
  • We do not store any copies of your QR codes or the data they contain
  • All QR code generation happens locally on your device
  • We do not track individual users or their usage patterns

Technical Information

We may collect anonymous, aggregated information about how our website is accessed and used. This may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Time and date of access
  • Pages visited

This information is used solely for the purpose of improving our website and service. It cannot be used to identify individual users.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device. They help us understand how our website is being used and allow us to make improvements. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for website analytics and performance monitoring. These services may collect anonymous data about how users interact with our website. We ensure that any third-party service we use adheres to strict privacy standards.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
